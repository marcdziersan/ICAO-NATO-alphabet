📖 About the ICAO / NATO / IMO Spelling Alphabet

The International Radiotelephony Spelling Alphabet, commonly known as the NATO phonetic alphabet, is the most widely used spelling alphabet worldwide.

This standardized alphabet ensures clear and unambiguous communication in critical environments where radio transmissions may be distorted or misunderstood.

✈️

ICAO

International Civil Aviation Organization

Developed the alphabet for global aviation communication.

🛡️

NATO

North Atlantic Treaty Organization

Adopted and standardized the alphabet for military operations.

🚢

IMO

International Maritime Organization

Uses the same alphabet for maritime communication.
